This is a fun burst bubble game! In this you have to hit a bubble to burst that,
and if you hit outside the bubble a new bubble will be generated. Your scores will be calculated based
on the number of bubbles you hit! As the time increases, the speed of the bubble will be
increased. When 75% of your screen gets filled with bubbles, the timer will start for
10 seconds. Then your game will end. You will have the pause button
on the top left corner and your scores will be update in the top left corner!
